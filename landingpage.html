VARIETIES

Plantain Banana

A versatile banana variety, great for cooking and frying. Plantains are larger and more starchy.

Cavendish Banana

The most common banana variety, known for its sweet flavor and soft texture. Ideal for eating raw.

Red Banana

Red bananas have a sweet, creamy flesh with a hint of raspberry flavor. Their red skin turns purple as they ripen.

Blue Java Banana

Known as the 'ice cream banana', Blue Java bananas have a creamy texture and vanilla-like flavor.

Apple Banana

These bananas are smaller and have an apple-like flavor. They are sweet and ideal for desserts.

Baby Banana

Baby bananas are smaller and sweeter than regular bananas. Perfect for snacking or adding to smoothies.
